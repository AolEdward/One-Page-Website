About our Band

Formed in Edmonton, Alberta. Five music enthusiast and have passion for music. Brought together for their love of sharing music.
From different genres of music, They come up with a new band in the community that sparked others to perform as well.
Edward (Guitars) has been playing in the past 5 years, doing crazy guitar solo and loud rock riffs.
Jeff (Bass) has been rocking with old bandmates, sets the groove and punchy bass performance.
Sherwin (Drums) has been a great addition, hence the band has been looking for a drummer that can keep up with energy performance.
Donna ( Vocals) is a premier voice in the community. Including her stunning looks and charm adds to the energy to the whole band.
Erwin (Keyboard) spices things with his extra ordinary sounds and focused keyboard playing.
